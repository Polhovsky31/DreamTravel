Our story

DreamTravel is an unusual travel company. Not only do we sell active tours in Russia, but we make every effort to develop the travel industry. Let’s tell you how it works and how you can help.

Our collection contains more than 150 routes through Russia. Kamchatka, Altai, Baikal, Putoran Plateau, Shantara Islands and dozens more places everyone dreams of. UNESCO World Heritage Sites, the most beautiful mountains, lakes and rivers. Places to meet right whales and polar bears, catch the northern lights and see the volcano erupt.

All these routes we check on ourselves. In our team are real travel experts - people who have climbed Elbrus, have reached the North Pole and have explored the most protected corners of Russia.

The safety of travel with RussiaDiscovery is confirmed by the Certificate of Registration of Tour Operators and numerous reviews of our tourists. Each of us can name 5 sights of Paris at once. But can you list 5 best places on Altai or Baikal? We want to make Russian travel a real trend. There are hundreds of incredible places in our country: lotus valleys, geyser valleys, colorful rock valleys, mountain towers, Buddhist temples, and even Stonehenge, built by nature.
