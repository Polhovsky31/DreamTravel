Our services

Tutu.ru

An online travel service which has been in operation since 2003. It helps to organise a comfortable trip from home. There are many relevant articles on the choice of route, taking into account the season and the current situation of the pandemic. On the site you can buy not only train, train, plane and bus tickets, book a hotel and buy a tour, but also choose a unique trip in the section «Adventure». Experienced travellers know the directions you are interested in and offer author’s tours in a fun company. They have detailed routes, a list of hotels and attractions - right up to the best places for selfies.

Youtravel.me

The Service, which also provides a huge selection of author’s group and individual tours in Russia. On the platform already almost 5,000 trips. and 2,200 organizers. The service is intended for those who are not interested in «packet» tours and want fresh emotions and more unique experience. Youtravel.me offers yoga tours, jeeps or flights to AN-2. During the journey, you can learn to cook a fishing ear, give an ayurvedic foot massage, make souvenirs with your own hands or learn Karelian music. Tours are selected by activity level, average age of the group and other individual criteria.

Альтруизм

It offers a new form of travel to the Russian interior, an alternative to tourism. The goal of the project is to develop small towns and villages of Russia with the help of volunteers from other regions, combining tours with volunteer activities. It is not just a journey, but a journey to implement projects of importance to local residents. For example, to restore the terem in the Kostroma region, to restore the stop in the village or to build an ecotrope in the reserve for people with disabilities. How cool is that?

Tripadvisor

It is one of the largest tourist sites in the world. The main utility of the service is the reviews of travelers about everything in the world: cities, sights, hotels, restaurants, excursions and so on. The Triathweiser is a giant travel community where you can find and leave feedback about almost any tourist service. Before going to a restaurant or booking a hotel, check the feedback on tripadvisor to avoid disappointment, waste and waste of time.
