Meet the team

We are here to create lasting memories for you because we use our expertise and time to ensure that you get the very best from your holiday. We’ve been doing this since 1996 and have a dynamic, creative team of experts right across the business to make your experience enjoyable from the moment you see one of our holidays to when you return. Our team, like our tours are worldwide as in every location and we recruit the most passionate and knowledgeable guides to bring your holiday to life with local insights and tips. People come back and travel with us every year as they know they’ll see and experience so much more on a Travel Department Holiday.
